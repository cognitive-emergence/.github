# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

- **Discuss the work, not the person.**
- **Prefer questions over declarations.** If you think something is wrong, ask how it was derived.
- **No protocol comparison.** We do not rank, rate, or dismiss other standards. We describe what problem we solve.
- **No marketing language.** Avoid "best," "first," "only." Use precise technical descriptions.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Personal attacks, harassment, or discriminatory language
- Promoting proprietary solutions under the guise of contribution
- Asserting legal compliance or liability guarantees on behalf of the project

## Enforcement

Maintainers have final authority. Violations result in warning or removal. Report privately to signal@humanjudgment.org.
